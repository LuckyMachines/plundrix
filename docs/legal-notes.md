# Plundrix Legal Notes

Current intended product posture:

- Sepolia staging is live
- mainnet is not live yet
- planned mainnet launch is free-play beta
- no cash prizes or monetary rewards are currently live
- the contract contains a configurable fee mechanism, but it should remain disabled on mainnet at launch
- Sepolia may enable fee settings for testing only

User-facing claims should stay aligned with this posture:

- do not describe the game as gambling
- do not describe the game as a sweepstakes, contest, or prize pool product
- do not imply users can win money
- do not imply Lucky Machines funds winnings
- do not market fee testing on Sepolia as a live economy

Operational notes:

- players may still pay normal blockchain gas fees for on-chain transactions
- smart contract interactions remain subject to normal wallet, network, and transaction risks
- agent and bot participation should be disclosed clearly where relevant

This file is a product-claims reference for the repo. It is not legal advice.
