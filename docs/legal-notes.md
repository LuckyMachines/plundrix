# Plundrix Legal Notes

Current intended product posture:

- Sepolia staging is live
- mainnet is not live yet
- the game supports two modes: FREE and STAKES
- FREE mode: no entry fees, no prizes, free-play
- STAKES mode: entry fees fund a player-contributed prize pool, winner takes pot minus a 2% protocol fee
- STAKES mode is a skill-based competition where outcomes are determined by player strategy (action selection, timing, tool management)
- the 2% protocol fee covers operational costs including autoloop gas
- mainnet may launch with STAKES mode enabled pending legal review

User-facing claims should stay aligned with this posture:

- do not describe the game as gambling, sweepstakes, or lottery
- STAKES mode should be framed as a skill-based competition with player-funded prize pools
- do not imply that outcomes involve chance beyond the normal variability of competitive play
- do not imply Lucky Machines funds winnings; prize pools come entirely from player entry fees
- do not market fee testing on Sepolia as a live economy
- emphasize that skill (action selection, timing, tool management) determines outcomes

Operational notes:

- players may still pay normal blockchain gas fees for on-chain transactions
- smart contract interactions remain subject to normal wallet, network, and transaction risks
- agent and bot participation should be disclosed clearly where relevant
- the marketing site should expose public Terms and Privacy pages
- Privacy copy should reflect actual site behavior such as waitlist email collection and optional analytics tooling when enabled

This file is a product-claims reference for the repo. It is not legal advice.
